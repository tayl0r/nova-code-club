NOVA Code Club

Grades 6 – 8

Build Your Own Web Game Using AI

Every student (or team of students) gets their own game project. No coding experience needed. Describe what you want to build, and watch AI bring it to life — right in front of you.

How It Works

📝

Dream it up

Write your game ideas in a Google Doc during the week — anything goes.

⚡

Build it live

Each Thursday, we feed your ideas to AI and build your game in real-time.

🤝

Collaborate

Work together with the group — suggest features, solve problems, level up each other's games.

🎨

Art & sound

Create your own or ask AI to generate using basic primitives. 2D, 3D, pixel art, anything goes!

🎮

Play your game

Every week your latest game build is deployed to GitHub where anyone can play it.
